The Studio

A studio thatkeeps the cityawake

Insomnia Murals began in a warehouse at 3 a.m. with one ladder, two cans and a wall nobody wanted. A decade later we paint landmarks, and build the brands behind them.

We turn blank concrete into the thing a city points at.

We’re a small, senior team of muralists, designers and producers. No account managers, no telephone game. You work with the people holding the cans.

Half our work is paint and lifts; the other half is identity: the logo, type and system that lives on the wall and everywhere else the brand shows up. We think the two should be designed together.

10+Years sleepless

One can. The whole language.

Our house aerosol, matte black with a paper label and a single red cap, is the studio in miniature. High-pressure for fills, low for fades, and a steady hand for the line that makes it ours.

Four things we refuse to compromise.

Nocturnal craft

We do our best thinking after dark. Quiet streets, long exposures, and the patience a great wall demands.

Scale without fear

Ten stories or ten feet. We plan, permit and produce work that holds up at architectural size.

Color with intent

Mostly monochrome, one decisive accent. Restraint is what makes the red land.

Built to last

UV-stable systems, sealed surfaces, documented and maintained. Landmarks, not billboards.

Bring us your most impossible wall.
